Section 1

Where else can you get top-notch education and perfect beach weather? At UCSD, you can finish your quantum physics homework while watching a sunset at La Jolla Shores. Plus, we’ve got Triton tacos that will change your life. You’ll find world-class research and a squirrel population that’s suspiciously friendly. So, whether you’re here for the academics, the beach, or just to figure out why we have a giant talking bear on campus (seriously, it's a thing), welcome to UCSD - where nerds go to thrive and tan.

Section 2

Revelle College, the oldest, embraces a rigorous liberal arts education, encouraging students to explore a wide range of disciplines. Muir College, in contrast, emphasizes an experiential learning approach, promoting the idea of "learning by doing" while supporting a strong connection to nature. Marshall College prioritizes diversity and global citizenship, urging students to think critically about societal issues. Warren College focuses on the integration of science and engineering with social responsibility, ensuring that students understand the ethical implications of their work.
